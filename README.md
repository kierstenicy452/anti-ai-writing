## 📝 How to use the editor

The interface is simple. You see a box on the left side of the window. Copy the text you want to edit. Paste it into this box. The software analyzes your text for common AI markers. It searches for specific words like "delve," "unlock," "leverage," and "harness." It also looks for complex sentence structures that sound mechanical.

Once you paste your text, the program runs a scan. Please wait a few seconds for the analysis. The screen then splits into two parts. The right side shows your rewritten text. This text uses simpler words and varied sentence lengths. 

Below the rewritten text, you see a diagnosis box. This box highlights the specific parts of your original writing that triggered the AI warnings. It explains why the software changed those words. You can copy the final version by clicking the "Copy" button.

## 🛠️ Supported features

This tool targets thousands of known patterns used by language models. Key features include:

* Word Removal: It replaces over 50 buzzwords that often flag AI content.
* Phrase Correction: It targets empty phrases like "in today's fast-paced world."
* Structural Fixes: It removes em dashes and prevents the "rule of three" pattern which frequent AI users recognize.
* Bilingual Support: Switch between English and Spanish in the settings menu.
* Accuracy Reports: Each scan provides a score based on how much the text resembles human writing styles.

## 🛡️ Privacy and data

Your privacy remains a priority. This software functions locally on your machine. The text you paste into the application stays on your computer. The software does not send your drafts to an external cloud server. You remain the only person with access to your documents. No files get uploaded to the internet during the rewriting process.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting common issues

If the application fails to open:

* Antivirus software sometimes flags new programs. If you cannot open the app, temporarily disable your antivirus software and try again.
* Ensure you installed the latest version from the link. Older versions may not work with new Windows updates.
* If the app freezes, close it using the Task Manager by pressing Control, Alt, and Delete. Restart the application.

## 📑 Understanding the diagnosis

The tool provides a diagnosis to help you improve your writing habits. It shows the count of repetitive structures. If you notice a high count, verify the rewritten text to see how the software improved the flow. You may notice less use of formal, hollow language. The goal is to make your content sound like a person wrote it, not a machine. Feel free to edit the suggested output before you finalize your document.
